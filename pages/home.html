putting eSwatini on the map

mapping for development #vision2022

what is #mapSwazi?
#mapSwazi is an initiative to improve map quality in the Kingdom of eSwatini, using the OpenStreetMap platform. It aims to (a) organize the contribution of eSwatini map data, and (b) to build a community of volunteer mappers.
why do we map?
  • to promote development
  • to improve navigation
  • because it's fun & cool!
  • read more our about page!
how you can help
The best way to help is by mapping some of the active projects! For now, this is more accessible for experienced mappers but soon we hope to have instructions for beginners.
